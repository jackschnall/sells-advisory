For Sellers

It’s your business, and we take it personally.

Sells specializes in mergers and acquisitions for Main Street, where founders and leaders of private businesses stand poised to write the next chapters in their stories.

This focus is by design. In these defining moments, our clients want an advisor who works to protect their priorities and preserve their legacies.

When the time is right, you’ll be ready.

There are many reasons why you might consider stepping away, stepping aside, or staying where you are. We understand the journey that led you to this moment, and we’ll guide you through whatever is next.

Retirement Planning

  • Convert years of hard work into financial security.
  • Maximize business value before you step away.
  • Know when to start planning for retirement, and when to exit.
  • Protect your legacy with a succession plan.
  • Create retirement income with tax-efficient strategies.
  • Choose a total sale, a gradual transition, a family succession plan, or a third-party buyer.

Growth Capital

  • Access capital without giving up control.
  • Fund expansion, new locations, or equipment and maintain majority ownership.
  • Bring in strategic, value-added partners with unique expertise.
  • Recapitalize to avoid a full exit, or maintaining status quo.
  • Solicit minority investment to help streamline operations and boost enterprise value.

Business Exit

  • Realize the utmost value for your life’s work.
  • Get your business sale-ready to help ensure the best possible outcome.
  • Explore the differences between strategic and financial buyers.
  • Consider the deal structure: all cash, earnouts, or seller notes.
  • Ensure a smooth transition for employees and customers.
  • Choose a full liquidity event and move on to the next chapter.

Partial Cash-out

  • Take chips off the table, but stay in the game.
  • Enjoy upside potential while protecting your personal wealth.
  • Bring on a new partner and take a second bite at the apple.
  • Gain liquidity for personal diversification without making a full exit.
  • Recapitalize and stay on as CEO/operator with fresh resources.
  • Roll equity positions and create value with institutional backing.

The Sells™ Process

Preparation and Positioning

Phase 1

Week 1–4

The Sells Process™ — Day One to Close

  • Conduct thorough diligence across financials, operations, customers, and teams
  • Develop top-tier CIM, teaser, and financial model; prep dataroom
  • Align on buyer outreach strategy and valuation framework

Phase 2

Week 5–8

Market Outreach & Engagement

  • Confidential outreach to pre-qualified strategic and financial buyers
  • Management of NDAs and distribution of marketing materials
  • Facilitation of management presentations and site visits
  • Collection and analysis of Indications of Interest (IOIs)

Phase 3

Week 9–16

Due Diligence & Negotiations

  • Coordination of financial, legal, operational, and environmental due diligence
  • Negotiation of Letter of Intent (LOI) terms
  • Purchase agreement drafting and redlining
  • Working capital and adjustment mechanism negotiations

Phase 4

Week 16–20

Close & Transition

  • Final purchase agreement execution
  • Funds flow coordination and closing mechanics
  • Transition planning support and communication strategy
  • Post-closing adjustment management

When it matters most, Trust Sells.

Sells is the middle-market investment bank and advisory firm people trust. We specialize in mergers and acquisitions for Main Street, where founders, owners and operators of private businesses can find investors, buyers, and sponsors who align with their priorities and share the desire to create lasting value.

We honor the human element in every transaction. Our approach starts with deep relationships, honest conversations, and a commitment to understanding what matters most to the people we serve.

Above all, we value trust.

Without trust, we’re simply doing transactions. Without trust, none of this works. So, everything we do helps create and support an environment of trust where people feel safe, confident, and free to pursue mutually rewarding outcomes. We believe trust happens when we follow three principles:

Dignity Empathy

Respect for all leads to deeper understanding and connection.

2

Transparency and communication lead to shared interests and cooperation.

Integrity Process

Discipline and precision lead to consistent, repeatable results.
