The Sells™ Process

Phase 1: Preparation & Positioning — Weeks 1–4

What to expect:

  • Comprehensive business review and valuation analysis
  • Financial model development and quality of earnings preparation
  • Marketing materials: Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM), executive summary, and teaser
  • Buyer universe development and strategic targeting
  • Entity and tax structure review with your advisors
Phase 2: Market Outreach — Weeks 5–8

What to expect:

  • Confidential outreach to pre-qualified strategic and financial buyers
  • Management of Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)
  • Distribution of marketing materials and data room access
  • Facilitation of management presentations and site visits
  • Collection and analysis of Indications of Interest (IOIs)
Phase 3: Due Diligence & Negotiations — Weeks 9–16

What to expect:

  • Coordination of financial, legal, operational, and environmental due diligence
  • Negotiation of Letter of Intent (LOI) terms
  • Purchase agreement drafting and redlining
  • Working capital and adjustment mechanism negotiations
  • Employment, non-compete, and transition planning
Phase 4: Close & Transition — Weeks 16–20

What to expect:

  • Final purchase agreement execution
  • Funds flow coordination and closing mechanics
  • Transition planning support and communication strategy
  • Post-closing adjustment management
  • Ongoing relationship and advisory support

When it matters most, Trust Sells.

Sells Advisory is a middle-market investment bank dedicated to helping founders, owners, and investors navigate the most important transactions of their lives. We were founded on a simple but powerful belief: that trust is the foundation of every successful relationship and transaction.

Our team brings together decades of experience across investment banking, private equity, finance, and entrepreneurship. We understand both sides of the table because we've sat in both seats. That perspective allows us to create an environment where open conversations lead to exceptional outcomes.

We don't just close deals—we guide people through defining moments with care, expertise, and integrity. Every engagement is built on the principles that define who we are: empathy for sellers, alignment with buyers, and a process that brings it all together.

Above all, we value trust.

Trust isn't just a word in our name—it's the foundation of everything we do. Our approach is built on three core principles that guide every relationship and transaction.

Seller Empathy

We begin every engagement by deeply understanding the seller—their goals, their fears, their non-negotiables. By leading with empathy, we create an environment of psychological safety that allows founders to make their best decisions with confidence and clarity.

Buyer Alignment

Clear Process

Our process is transparent, efficient, and meticulously managed. From preparation through close, every step is designed to protect your interests, minimize disruption, and deliver outcomes that exceed expectations. No surprises, no shortcuts, no gaps.
