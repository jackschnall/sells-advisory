### 1. Hero Photos Are Placeholders on Most Pages
Every interior page (For Buyers, About/Company, Our Difference, Leadership, Insights, Blog Post, Contact) shows a gray "Photo placeholder" box where the PDF has actual photographs. Only the Home page and For Sellers page have real hero photos. The following hero photos need to be added:
- **For Buyers hero**: PDF shows a warehouse/industrial scene with workers
- **Our Difference hero**: PDF shows a close-up of a rubber stamp/seal on a desk
- **Leadership hero**: PDF shows a desk scene with coffee mug, notebook, and Sells coaster
- **Insights hero**: PDF shows a hand holding a tablet/phone
- **Blog Post hero**: PDF shows same tablet/phone image as Insights
- **Contact Us hero**: PDF shows two men looking at a tablet (same photo as used in the For Sellers "right people" section and Let's Talk sections)

### 2. Placeholder Photos Throughout Content Sections
Beyond heroes, many content sections show gray placeholder boxes instead of actual photos:
- **For Buyers** intro section (PDF shows two men, one older with beard talking)
- **For Buyers** "Why buyers trust Sells" section (PDF shows the same two men from a different angle)
- **Company page** "Above all, we value trust" section photo (PDF shows two businessmen talking)
- **Company page** "The right people" section photo (PDF shows two men, one with laptop)
- **Our Difference** "What the Sells Difference delivers for Sellers" section photo (PDF shows industrial/manufacturing scene)

- **Leadership** "The right people" section photo

### 3. Text Content Differences on Multiple Pages
Several pages have body copy that doesn't match the PDF. While some differences may be intentional (replacing lorem ipsum with real copy), many appear to be unintended rewrites. See page-specific sections below for exact text comparisons.
